12.13.17

Deployment SUCCESSFUL

Welcome to I GOT YOUR SIX! This website is a new venture for a not so serious clan. We play a ton of games that you can find out about HERE. If you would like to contact us we are all on XBOX live. Simply send us an message if you would like to join the clan or if you have any other questions. You can get a list of the current members and their gamertags in the ABOUT section of our website.

I'd like to take a few minutes to flesh out for everyone what this site is geared towards. My name is Trace (totalDepth) and I created this site initially as a project for my web-development portfolio, but I am also wanting to keep it as an on going experience. This site won't necessarily be a blog of sorts, but more about changes to Destiny 2 and my progress toward launching a web application that uses the Destiny API that Bungie provides developers. I will try to post information I find throughout playing Destiny 2 and what's included in the weekly resets and seasonal resets. I am currently making my way (slowly) through the expansion Curse of Osiris. I'm not an everyday player, but I have a ton of fun playing with the clan. On the Home page you'll find a section for a list of the weekly Xur items and his location. You'll also find the weekly Flashpoint location and what the Nightfall Strike is as well as its quirks. I'll be posting updates as the weeks come about what I will be doing with the web-application. Think of it as a web version of the Destiny 2 companion app for IOS and Android.
